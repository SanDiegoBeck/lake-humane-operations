Since 1937, the Lake Humane Society has been dedicated to the welfare and protection of animals.
Operating as a private, non-profit organization in Lake County, Ohio and the surrounding metropolitan areas, we are focused on the relief of suffering and the prevention of cruelty and abuse to animals. We also strive to inform and educate children and adults in the humane and respectful treatment of all living creatures. Please consider donating. Your donation will help save an animals life!
